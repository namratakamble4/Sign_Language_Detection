About Sign Language


Sign language is a visual language that uses hand gestures, body language, and facial expressions to communicate.


It is the primary language used by individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing, and it varies across the world, including languages like American Sign Language (ASL), British Sign Language (BSL), and others.

About Our App


Our Sign Language Detection app is designed to bridge the communication gap for those who rely on sign language.


Using advanced AI technology, the app translates sign language gestures into text in real time, making communication easier and more accessible for everyone.
